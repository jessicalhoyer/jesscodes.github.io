Wild Equines RPG

Summary

Wild Equines RPG is a collaborative writing website where members create their own characters and write them with other members using forum posts. The forum has been running since 2007 and encompasses members from all over the world. In 2011, I became an administrator of the website.


As an administrator, I handle all kinds of things around the site, including writing site updates and announcements, handling member disputes, implementing site changes, and keeping up site maintenance. In addition, I also design the site layouts and color schemes using the existing Proboards framework.
